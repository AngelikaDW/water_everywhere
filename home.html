Fresh Water Drinking Fontains in Amsterdam

Fresh water to tap in Amsterdam

Buying bottled water is lame and bad for the environment, but staying hydrated and refreshed is cool and good for you.

Still bottled water is sold in stunning numbers: in Amsterdam 21.7m liters of water was sold in bottles in 2014
(Source AMS Instituut voor stadsonderzoek)

Luckly there are public water taps all around Amsterdam (courtesy of Waternet) offering fresh sweet water to fill your bottle and or to sip from.

The drinkwater fontains tend to blend so well into the city, you might have trouble finding them.

So this site gives you an overview where to find the nearest water fountain to quench your thirst.

Keep hydrated and happily exploring Amsterdam!
