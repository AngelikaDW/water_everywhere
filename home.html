Fresh water to tap in Amsterdam

Buying bottled water is lame and bad for the environment, but staying hydrated and refreshed is cool and good for you.

Luckly there are public water taps all around Amsterdam (courtesy of Waternet) offering fresh sweet water to fill your bottle and or to sip from. However they tend to blend so well into the city, you might have trouble finding them.

So this site gives you an overview and hopefully soon I will know enough of dynamic webpages to enable you to search for the closest tap near you to quench your thirst.

Keep hydrated and happily exploring Amsterdam!
