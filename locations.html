Fresh water tap in Amsterdam

Find the nearest water fontain using the map or in the list below!

The locations are approximate. There are two different types of water fountains:

  • "Delta-Tap": block shaped fixtures made of stainless steel. Push the butn at the p activate the water flow.
  • "Happertjes" A basic premise sip from: just a continuous stream of water that shoots up in the air and falls back in its own basin.

Keep hydrated and happily exploring Amsterdam!
